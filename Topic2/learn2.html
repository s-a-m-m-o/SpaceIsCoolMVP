Stars

Some quick cosmic facts!


  • Most stars are considered to be "red dwarves"

  • They twinkle because of movement in the Earth's atmosphere

  • The smaller they are the longer they live
    (although even massive stars still can live for billions of years)

  • The sun is a star and is about 4.5 billion years old!

  • The closest star to the sun is about 4.2 light-years away!


Categories of stars

  • Small stars are red and are duller

  • Medium sized stars are yellow, an example of one would be the Sun

  • Large stars are blue in colour and are extremely bright
